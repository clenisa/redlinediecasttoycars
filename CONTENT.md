## üöó Diecast Cars & Product Information

### 9. What is die-cast?
Die-cast refers to **model cars made with a metal body**, typically using **zinc alloy**. Some diecast models may also include plastic parts for details, but the core structure is **high-quality metal**.

### 10. What scale sizes do you offer?
We carry various **diecast model scales**, including:
- **1/12 Scale** ‚Äì About **13"‚Äì15" inches long**
- **1/18 Scale** ‚Äì About **10"‚Äì12" inches long**
- **1/24 Scale** ‚Äì About **7"‚Äì8" inches long**
- **1/32 Scale** ‚Äì About **4"‚Äì5" inches long**
- **1/64 Scale** ‚Äì About **3" inches long**

Each product description includes **exact dimensions** for reference.

### 11. Do all products come in the manufacturer's packaging?
Yes, all items are **brand new** and come in their **original manufacturer packaging**. However, **manufacturers may change the design of the packaging over time**, but the models remain the same.

# Return Policy

At **Redline Diecast Toy Cars**, customer satisfaction is our priority. If you need to **return or exchange an item**, please carefully review our policy below to ensure a smooth process.

## Returns & Exchanges

- All **returns or exchanges** must be **reported within 30 days** from the date of purchase.
- If your item arrives **damaged or defective**, you must **notify us immediately upon receipt** so we can file a claim with the shipping carrier. **If we are not notified right away, we will not be able to accept the item for a return or exchange.**
- Merchandise must be **returned in its original factory condition, in the original box, and packaged exactly as the manufacturer packed it.**

## Important Notes on Returns

- **Do not attempt to fix or glue any broken parts** without our authorization. **Items that have been glued or tampered with will not be accepted for return.**
- Items **without their original packaging** or that show signs of alteration will not be eligible for a return.
- If you are using a **third-party shipping carrier** to forward your package outside the **USA**, we are **not responsible** for any damage incurred during transit.

## Shipping & Handling Fees

- **Shipping and handling fees are non-refundable** unless Redline Diecast Toy Cars has made an error by shipping an incorrect item.
- If we shipped an incorrect item, you must **contact us within 7 days of delivery** to request a **return label** or **merchandise pickup**. Once we receive the incorrect item at our warehouse, we will either ship the **correct item** or issue a **full refund** if the correct item is unavailable.

## Unclaimed, Undeliverable, and Refused Deliveries

- Orders that are **unclaimed, undeliverable, or refused** will be **canceled and refunded**, **minus the original shipping cost.**

## How to Start a Return

- Prior to any return, **you must notify us immediately upon receipt at** redlinediecasttoysstore@gmail.com with the reason for the return.
- If you experience any issues with your order, **contact us right away at** redlinediecasttoysstore@gmail.com, and we will be happy to assist.

For further assistance, please reach out to our **customer support team at** redlinediecasttoysstore@gmail.com or via our live chat, and we will ensure that your return process is handled smoothly.
